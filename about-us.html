About Novodente

Our Introduction

At Novodente, we are committed to providing a new kind of dental care that prioritises your comfort, confidence, and long-term oral health. We work collaboratively with highly skilled clinicians and lab technicians to restore your smile and improve your oral health.

Led by Dr Roshwin Pereira Carvalho and Dr Arantxa Menezes, both with extensive experience, We work collaboratively with highly skilled clinicians and lab technicians to provide the best possible care.

Our Principal Dentists

Meet Roshwin and Arantxa

At Novodente, your care is led by two highly skilled and compassionate dental professionals with over 20 years of combined experience and well over a thousand dental implants placed and restored along with routine and cosmetic dentistry.

Together, Roshwin and Arantxa combine advanced training and extensive experience to deliver outstanding results, transforming smiles and lives with care and precision.

We believe in building strong relationships with our patients, guiding you through every step of your dental journey. Whether for a routine check-up or a smile makeover, we make sure you feel informed, comfortable, and empowered.

Dr Roshwin Pereira Carvalho

Dr Roshwin is renowned for his expertise in dental implants, having placed over a thousand of dental implants with exceptional precision. His dedication to innovation and patient-centred care ensures that you are always in expert hands.

Education

  • B.D.S. (Goa -India)
  • Diploma of Implant Dentistry, Royal College of Surgeons (Edinburgh)

Dr Arantxa Menezes

Dr Arantxas strength lie in restorative dentistry and orthodontics, offering tailored solutions to improve both the health and aesthetics of your smile. Her commitment to excellence and her empathetic nature makes her a trusted partner in your dental journey.

Education

What Makes Novodente Unique

At Novodente, we are redefining the dental experience by combining innovation, personalised care, and a focus on patient comfort.

Cutting-Edge Technology

We use the latest advancements in dental technology like including CBCT imaging, 3D printing and CAD CAM design, for precise, efficient, and minimally invasive treatments.

Seamless Communication

Our advanced communication tools make scheduling, consultations, and progress tracking easy, offering a convenient way to manage your oral health.

Gentle, Stress-Free Treatments

We focus on creating a calm, welcoming environment with gentle techniques and a compassionate team, making every visit as relaxing as possible.

Comprehensive Services

From preventive care to cosmetic and restorative treatments, we look at your mouth as a whole.

Patient Education

We empower you with the knowledge to make informed decisions about your oral health, explaining procedures and treatment options clearly.

Long-Term Results

Our goal is not just short-term care—we're dedicated to helping you maintain lasting oral health and a smile you can be proud of for years to come.
