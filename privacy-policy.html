  2. Privacy Policy

Novodente ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, and other information you provide when booking an appointment.
  • Health Information: Information related to your dental history and treatment preferences.
  • Usage Data: Information about your visit, such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and manage appointments.
  • To provide and improve our dental services.
  • To communicate with you regarding appointments and promotions.
  • To comply with legal obligations and ensure website security.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, appointment scheduling platforms).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no online transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at dr.roshwin@gmail.com

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on our website.
